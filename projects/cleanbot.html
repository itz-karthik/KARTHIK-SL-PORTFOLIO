IoT Based Smart Toilet Cleaning Bot


  • It utilizes a compact robot equipped with ultrasonic and other advanced sensors, along with a brush mechanism, to automate the cleaning process in toilets. Once a person leaves the toilet, the sensors detect their absence and activate the robot to initiate cleaning, including flushing and scrubbing, ensuring a hygienic and spotless toilet.

  • The robot is integrated with a room freshener system, which automatically sprays a refresher after cleaning to eliminate odors and maintain a pleasant environment. This feature ensures that the toilet remains clean, odor-free, and suitable for subsequent users.

  • Designed for use in train toilets, the system aims to address the challenges of poor hygiene and irregular cleaning. By automating the cleaning process, it eliminates the need for manual intervention, locks the toilet door during cleaning for safety, and unlocks it once the process is complete, enhancing the overall user experience and hygiene standards.
