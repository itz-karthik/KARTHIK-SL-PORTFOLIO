Dynamic Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging System with integration of Square Coils


  • Our project enables wireless charging for electric vehicles by integrating advanced charging pads on road surfaces, allowing vehicles to charge effortlessly during both stationary and motion phases. This ensures an uninterrupted power supply, making the charging process more convenient and reducing reliance on traditional charging stations.

  • It utilizes the principle of magnetic induction, where energy is wirelessly transferred between coils in the charging pads and the vehicle. This innovative approach eliminates the need for physical connectors, ensuring a safer, more reliable, and maintenance-free charging experience.

  • Our project aims to transform the EV charging infrastructure by significantly reducing charging downtime and enabling continuous vehicle operation. By promoting this advanced technology, it paves the way for increased EV adoption and contributes to the development of a sustainable and eco-friendly transportation system.
